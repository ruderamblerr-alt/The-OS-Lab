The Habit Loop
Every habit, good or bad, is built from the same four-part cycle: a cue triggers a craving, the craving drives a response, and the response delivers a reward. Understanding this loop is the first step to designing habits that actually stick.
Notice It
the signal that starts it all
👀
Cue — the trigger
A cue is any signal — external or internal — that tells your brain it's time to start a habit. It's the spark that kicks off the whole loop.
Example
💧 Drink Water 2L — waking up, and every time you sit back down at your desk after being away.
